Hi, This is

Karma Patel

Data Science Enthusiast

Karma got his undergraduate Engineering degree from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT). Following his graduation, Karma joined Hong Kong and Shanghai Banking Corporation (HSBC) as a Software Engineer, working on Java, Python, SQL, Web Development Frameworks and Automation. At HSBC, he worked for the Commercial Banking (CMB) vertical and was a member of the GTRF Innovation Lab pioneer team. He was highly appreciated for his innovation initiatives and quality of work, which earned him a “Star Award”. Karma also worked within other roles, including Subject Matter Expert and Architect In-charge.

At HSBC, Karma’s work on a project on Wolfsberg Group (an association of thirteen global banks which aims to develop frameworks and guidance for the management of financial crime risks) allowed him a first look into data analytics. Following the project, Karma received praise for his keen understanding of the business processes, and the ability to find data patterns and link them to specific processes.

Inspired by the potential of data analytics, Karma joined the Business Analytics and Information Management program at the Purdue University. He firmly believes in using analytics as part of a process and not as an end in itself; for Karma, setting clear business questions and using analytics in the right place to reach the end solution is the way to go. At Krannert, he is learning business, quantitative and analytical tools to do just that. In the future, Karma looks to work on resolving business challenges using technology and analytics, where he can apply his passion in storytelling with data and numbers.
